About Us

Different souls stiched together. Yes, that's the shortest description that we could think of. We are a bunch of ex-students (ex? Really? In spirit, probably not!) students of the Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur located in the suburbs of the city of joy, Kolkata. Like most other students, we owe almost everything to Narendrapur. We studied Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Statistics, History, English, Economics. And yet, we speak the same language. Today, after many years of graduation, we are scattered all over the world, busy in as different professions as imaginable, we still speak the same language, laugh at jokes that are hardly above absolute rubbish, and of course, obscure to other mortals. We are insignificant people with significant amount of nonsense to talk about. This is our place to give vent to all of them.

It all started with Mahaguru, and Guruji. By the way, Gurukul was the first name to pop up and we said, "Why not"? Mahaguru and Guruji are therefore the guardians, the spearheads of this clique of ours. But other gurus are also equally important, and so are the gurumatas (be not mistaken, gurumatas are the wives/partners of gurus) and shisyas. Want to know more about us?
